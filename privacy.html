Privacy Policy

Prayer Help · Effective July 19, 2026

Prayer Help does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no server. Everything you enter — your prayer list, reflections, and settings — is stored only on your device and never leaves it.

Notifications

If you turn on the daily reminder, the notification is scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to us or anyone else.

Children

The app is designed to be safe for all ages. Because no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children.
