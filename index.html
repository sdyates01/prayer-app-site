Common questions

Where is my prayer list stored?
Everything — your prayer list, reflections, and settings — is stored only on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How do reminders work?
The daily reminder is a local notification scheduled on your device. You can turn it on or off and change the time in Settings.

I deleted the app — can I get my list back?
Because data lives only on the device, deleting the app deletes its data. There is no account or backup server.
